About Us

Welcome to Saithong Para Thai, where the heart of Thailand meets the warmth of Virginia. “Saithong” means golden light — a symbol of joy and hospitality that inspires everything we do. Saithong Para Thai, located in Fredericksburg, VA, is a beloved spot for authentic Thai cuisine. With a warm and inviting atmosphere, the restaurant offers a wide variety of flavorful dishes, from traditional curries to stir-fries. Guests enjoy excellent service, and the menu caters to various tastes and dietary preferences.

Whether you choose to dine in or take out, Saithong Para Thai promises a satisfying experience with fresh, delicious food.

Famous Thai Dishes

Pad See Ew

A popular stir-fried noodle dish made with wide rice noodles, soy sauce, Chinese broccoli, egg, and your choice of protein. Smoky, savory, slightly sweet.

Yellow Curry

Mild coconut curry with potatoes, perfect for those who enjoy deep flavor without too much heat.

Pad Thai

Iconic Thai noodle dish with tamarind, egg, peanuts, and bean sprouts.

Thai Tea Or Cha Yen
